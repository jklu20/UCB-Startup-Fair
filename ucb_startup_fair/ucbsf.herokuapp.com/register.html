Registration

Startups and portfolio companies around the world are welcome to register for the UCB Global Startup Fair.

Registrations will be handled via Eventbrite. Upon purchasing a ticket, your company's spot in the Fair will be reserved, and the registrant will be contacted by a representative from one of our organizations.

Our representative will work with you and your company leading up to, at, and after the Fair to ensure that you get the maximum benefit from our event.
